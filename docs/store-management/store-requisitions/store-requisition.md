# Store Requisition

Handle store supply requisitions directly from internal departments to the store.

## Store Requisition List
To review these requests:
1. Go to the **Store Requisition** module.
2. The system lists all requested internal transfers or supplies.

![Store Requisition List](/img/store/store-requisition/store-requisition-list.png)

## View Requisition Details
To view the contents of a particular requisition:
1. Click the view button next to the desired item.

![View Requisition](/img/store/store-requisition/store-requisition-view.png)

## Add a New Store Requisition
To request items from the store:
1. Click the **Add** button.
2. You can directly request items or associate related requests (sub-tasks).

![Add Store Requisition](/img/store/store-requisition/store-requisition-add.png)
