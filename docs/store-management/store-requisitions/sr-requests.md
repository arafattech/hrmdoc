# SR Requests

Track and trace Store Requisitions through their various stages and statuses.

## Submitted SR Requests
To look at recently submitted requests:
1. Open the **SR Requests** panel and navigate to the **Submitted** tab.

![Submitted Requests](/img/store/store-requisition/sr-equests/sr-requests-submitted.png)

## Approved SR Requests
To view requests that have passed the approval phase:
1. Navigate to the **Approved** tab.

![Approved Requests](/img/store/store-requisition/sr-equests/sr-requests-approved.png)

## Rejected SR Requests
To view requests that were denied:
1. Navigate to the **Rejected** tab.

![Rejected Requests](/img/store/store-requisition/sr-equests/sr-requests-rejected.png)

## SR Requests List View
To view granular specifics of an individual SR Request:
1. Click the eye action icon on any request item across any tab.

![SR Requests View](/img/store/store-requisition/sr-equests/sr-requests-list-view.png)
