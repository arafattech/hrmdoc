# Item Inventory

Manage and monitor all your stored inventory items seamlessly through this module.

## Item Inventory List
To see the full inventory list:
1. Open the **Item Inventory** module.
2. The view outlines all items along with their current stock levels.

![Item Inventory List](/img/store/item-inventory/item-inventory-list.png)

## View Inventory Details by Categories
To see specific category breakdowns such as Clients, Core, Plants, and Suppliers:
1. Select an inventory item and navigate through the relevant tabs or icons.
