# Store Analytics

This module gives you a detailed overview of your store analytics.

## View Analytics
To view store analytics:
1. Navigate to the **Store Analytics** section.
2. Review the data and charts presented on the dashboard.

![Analytics](/img/store/analytics/analytics.png)

## Filter Analytics
To apply filters to your analytics:
1. Locate the filter panel on the analytics page.
2. Set the desired parameters (date range, specific items, etc.).
3. The dashboard will automatically update with the filtered results.

![Analytics Filter](/img/store/analytics/analytics-filter.png)
