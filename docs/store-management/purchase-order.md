# Purchase Order

Track and manage incoming purchase orders to the store.

## Purchase Order List
To view all purchase orders:
1. Navigate to the **Purchase Order** menu.
2. The list displays active and previous PO operations.

![Purchase Order List](/img/store/purchase-order/purchase-order.png)

## View Purchase Order Details
To view details of a specific PO:
1. Click the 'view' action row icon.
2. Look through the required items and corresponding quantities.

![Purchase Order View](/img/store/purchase-order/purchase-order-view.png)
