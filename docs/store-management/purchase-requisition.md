# Purchase Requisition

Manage internal company requests for new purchases.

## Purchase Requisition List
To access the requisition lists:
1. Navigate to the **Purchase Requisition** section.
2. The table shows a full overview of logged requisitions.

![Purchase Requisition List](/img/store/purchase-requisition/purchaisition-list.png)

## Add a New Requisition
To create a new purchase requisition:
1. Click on the **Add New** button.
2. Enter the corresponding criteria and items to be purchased.

![Add Requisition](/img/store/purchase-requisition/purchaisition-add.png)

## View Requisition Details
To see a specific order's detailed view:
1. Click the viewing icon.
2. Check specific remarks, statuses, and requested items.

![View Requisition](/img/store/purchase-requisition/purchaisition-list-view.png)
