# Supplier Bank Details

The **Supplier Bank Details** module allows you to manage and verify the payment information of all registered suppliers. This ensures that financial transactions and bank transfers are processed correctly securely.

## 1. Viewing All Bank Details

To view every supplier bank details record in the system:
1. Navigate to **Base Data Management > Supplier Bank Details**.
2. Click on the **All** tab.

![All Supplier Bank Details](/img/basedata/subpplier-bank-details/supplier-bank-details-all.png)

This view provides a comprehensive list of all entries. You can see the **Supplier Name**, **Mode of Payment** (e.g., Cash, Bank Transfer), **Bank Name**, **Account Number**, and **Creation/Verification timestamps**.

## 2. Managing Non-Verified Records

New or updated bank details often require verification before they are fully active.

1. Click on the **Non-Verified** tab to filter the list to only those records awaiting approval. This helps managers easily identify pending tasks.

![Non-Verified Bank Details](/img/basedata/subpplier-bank-details/supplier-bank-details-non-verified.png)

2. To review, verify, or edit a pending record, click the blue **Edit (Pencil) icon** located in the "Actions" column.

![Edit Non-Verified Bank Details](/img/basedata/subpplier-bank-details/supplier-bank-details-non-verified-edit.png)

3. Update any necessary banking information, adjust the verification status, and save your changes.

## 3. Viewing Verified Bank Details

To check which suppliers are fully approved and ready for transactions:
1. Click on the **Verified** tab.

![Verified Bank Details](/img/basedata/subpplier-bank-details/supplier-bank-details.png)

In this tab, all entries will display a checkmark (✅) indicating their payment details have been successfully verified. 

## 4. Editing Verified Bank Details

If you need to update the payment information for an already verified supplier:
1. From either the **Verified** tab or the **All** tab, click the blue **Edit (Pencil) icon** next to the relevant supplier record.

![Edit Verified Bank Details](/img/basedata/subpplier-bank-details/supplier-bank-details-edit.png)

2. Make required adjustments to the payment mode, account number, or routing details, and submit the changes.
