# Department

The `Department` module is used to manage department records in the system. It allows users to view the department list, create new departments, update existing records, and delete unused departments.

## Department List

![Department List](/img/basedata/department/department-list.png)

Go to `Base Data Management > Department` to open the department list page.

### Information Shown on This Page

- **Active**: Shows whether the department is active.
- **Custom ID**: Displays the department code or custom ID.
- **Name**: Displays the department name.
- **Created On**: Shows the date when the department record was created.
- **Actions**: Provides options to edit or delete the department.

### Top Controls

- **Search**: Use this to find a department by name or custom ID.
- **New**: Opens the form to add a new department.

## Add a New Department

![Add Department](/img/basedata/department/department-add.png)

Follow these steps to add a new department:

1. Go to `Base Data Management > Department`.
2. Click the **New** button at the top of the page.
3. Keep the **Active** toggle on if the department should remain active.
4. Enter the department name in the **Department Name** field.
5. Click **Submit** to save the department.
6. Click **Cancel** if you do not want to save.

### Field Description

- **Active**: Saves the department as active or inactive.
- **Department Name**: The name of the department. This field is required.

## Edit a Department

![Edit Department](/img/basedata/department/department-edit.png)

Follow these steps to edit a department:

1. Find the required department in the list.
2. Click the **Edit** icon in the **Actions** column.
3. Update the **Active** status or **Department Name** as needed.
4. Click **Submit** to save the changes.
5. Click **Cancel** to discard the changes.

## Delete a Department

![Delete Department](/img/basedata/department/department-delete.png)

Follow these steps to delete a department:

1. Find the department you want to delete from the list.
2. Click the **Delete** icon in the **Actions** column.
3. In the confirmation popup, click **Delete** to remove the record.
4. Click **Cancel** if you do not want to continue.

:::warning
If a department may still be needed for reporting or existing records, review the impact before deleting it.
:::
