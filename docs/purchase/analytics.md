# Purchase Analytics

This module provides a comprehensive overview of purchase-related analytics. You can use it to track and analyze your purchasing data efficiently.

## View Analytics
To view your purchase analytics:
1. Navigate to the **Purchase Analytics** module from the main menu.
2. The dashboard will display key metrics and charts regarding your purchasing activities.

![Analytics](/img/purchase/analytics/analytices.png)

## Filter Analytics
To filter the analytics data:
1. On the Analytics page, locate the filter options.
2. Select your desired criteria (e.g., date range, department, or specific parameters).
3. The dashboard will automatically update to reflect the filtered results.

![Analytics Filter](/img/purchase/analytics/analytices-filter.png)
