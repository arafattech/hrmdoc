# SPR Requests

This module allows you to manage Store Purchase Requisition (SPR) requests, including viewing approved and rejected requests.

## View Item Details
To view the detailed breakdown of an SPR request:
1. Click on the appropriate icon or link next to an SPR request in the list.
2. The item details view provides detailed information about the requested items and their status.

![SPR Requests Item](/img/purchase/spr-requests/spr-requests-item.png)

## Overview of Approved SPR Requests
To manage approved SPR requests:
1. Navigate to the **Approved** tab within the SPR Requests module.
2. Here, you will see a list of all SPRs that have been approved along with their progress.

![Approved SPR Requests](/img/purchase/spr-requests/spr-requests-approved.png)

## View Approved Item Details
To view the specific breakdown of an approved SPR item:
1. Select an approved request from the list to open its specific details.
2. The breakdown shows approved quantities and corresponding parameters.

![Approved Item Details](/img/purchase/spr-requests/spr-requests-approved-item.png)

## Overview of Rejected SPR Requests
To review rejected SPR requests:
1. Navigate to the **Rejected** tab in the SPR Requests module.
2. You will see a list of SPRs that were denied by management or the approval authority.

![Rejected SPR Requests](/img/purchase/spr-requests/spr-requests-rejected.png)

## View Rejected Item Details
To examine why an SPR request was rejected:
1. Click on a specific rejected request to open its view panel.
2. This provides context on the rejection and lists the specific requested items.

![Rejected Item View](/img/purchase/spr-requests/spr-requests-rejected-view.png)
