# Purchase Order

The Purchase Order module enables you to manage, review, and create new purchase orders to restock items and manage external supplier requests.

## Purchase Orders List
To access your purchase orders:
1. Navigate to **Purchase Order** from the primary menu bar.
2. The overview tab provides a comprehensive list of all generated purchase orders alongside their quantities, vendor info, and current statuses.

![Purchase Orders](/img/purchase/purchase-orders/purchase-orders.png)

## View Purchase Order Details
To inspect the specific properties of an existing Purchase Order:
1. Click the view action button (eye icon) on any purchase order line item.
2. This displays all related details, item breakdowns, totals, and approval histories for that specific PO.

![Purchase Order View](/img/purchase/purchase-orders/purchase-orders-view.png)

## Create a New Purchase Order
To create a new Purchase Order directly:
1. Click the appropriate **Add New** or create button on the Purchase Orders overview page.
2. Input all required supplier details, specific item line-lines, expected dates, and monetary amounts.
3. Submit or save the form to permanently record the transaction.

![New Purchase Order](/img/purchase/purchase-orders/purchase-orders-new.png)
