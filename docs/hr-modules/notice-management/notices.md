# Notices Management

The **Notices** module empowers HR and Administrators to broadcast important announcements, policies, or holiday information to the entire organization, specific departments, or individual employees. It allows for rich text formatting, drafting capabilities, and tracking employee acknowledgements.

## Viewing Notices

The main page provides a clean overview of all notices:

- **S/N**: Serial number.
- **Title**: The subject of the notice.
- **Created On**: The date the notice was drafted or published.
- **Actions**: Options to View, Edit (for drafts), or Delete (for drafts).

You can easily switch between views using the top tabs:
- **All**: Shows every notice created.
- **Published**: Shows only notices that are live and visible to employees.
- **Draft**: Shows notices that are saved but not yet broadcasted.

![Notice List](/img/hr/notice-management/notice-list.png)

## Creating and Publishing a Notice

To broadcast a new notice:

1. Click the **New** button from the top right.
2. **Select Target Audience**: Choose who should receive this notice from the dropdown:
   - **All Employees**: Broadcasts to everyone in the system.
   - **Departments**: A second dropdown will appear allowing you to select specific departments.
   - **Individual Employees**: A second dropdown will appear allowing you to select specific people.
3. Enter a clear **Title**.
4. Use the rich text editor to write your **Content**. You can format text, add lists, links, or media.
5. Check **Require Acknowledgement** if employees must explicitly confirm they have read the notice.
6. Click **Published** to send it out immediately, or **Draft** to save it for later.

## Managing Drafts

If you saved a notice as a draft, it will not be visible to employees yet. You can manage these in the **Draft** tab.

![Notice Draft List](/img/hr/notice-management/notice-draft-list.png)

### Editing a Draft
To continue working on a notice:
1. Click the **Edit** icon next to the draft.
2. Make your necessary modifications to the audience, text, or acknowledgement requirement.
3. Click **Published** to finally make it live.

![Edit Notice Draft](/img/hr/notice-management/notice-draft-edit.png)

### Deleting a Draft
If a drafted notice is no longer needed:
1. Click the **Delete** icon next to the draft.
2. Confirm the action when the warning modal asks *"Are you sure you want to delete?"*

![Delete Notice Draft](/img/hr/notice-management/notice-draft-delete.png)

## Viewing Notice Details

To review the exact contents of a published notice or check who has acknowledged it:

1. Click the **View** icon in the Actions column.
2. The modal will display the **Notice Detail** (Title and Content).
3. Switch to the **Employee Feedback** tab to monitor acknowledgements (if the notice required them).

![View Notice Details](/img/hr/notice-management/notice-view.png)
