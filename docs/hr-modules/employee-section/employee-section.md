# Employee Section

The **Employee Section** module helps divide your company's workforce into logical, functional, or physical groups such as Electrical & Utility, Quality Assurance, Finishing, Transport, etc. Sections are essential for managing shifts, reports, and team-specific policies.

## Viewing Sections

The section list allows you to review all structural divisions setup in the system:

- **Active**: Shows active vs inactive status.
- **Name**: The distinct name of the section.
- **Created On**: The setup date in the system.
- **Actions**: Manage via **Edit** or **Delete**.

Filter views using the standard **All**, **Active**, and **Inactive** tabs, or use the top-right search box.

![Employee Section Overview](/img/hr/employee-section/employee-section.png)

## Adding a New Section

To create a new organizational section:

1. Click the **New** button.
2. In the modal:
   - Keep the **Active** toggle checked.
   - Input the **Section Name** (e.g., "Maintenance").
3. Click **Submit** to finalize.

![Add Employee Section](/img/hr/employee-section/employee-section-add.png)

## Editing a Section

To modify an existing section:

1. Click the **Edit** icon.
2. Change the section's name or toggle its active state.
3. Hit **Submit** to save changes.

![Edit Employee Section](/img/hr/employee-section/employee-section-edit.png)

## Viewing Inactive Sections

Sections that have been phased out can be found in the **Inactive** list.

1. Click the **Inactive** toggle tab at the top right.
2. You can reactivate any old section by editing it and turning the **Active** toggle back to on.

![Inactive Employee Section](/img/hr/employee-section/employee-section-inactive.png)

## Deleting a Section

To remove a section that was added by mistake or is completely obsolete:

1. Click the **Delete** button.
2. A warning box will appear indicating *"Are you sure you want to delete?"*
3. Click **Delete** to proceed.

![Delete Employee Section](/img/hr/employee-section/employee-section-delete.png)
