# Employee Leave Request

This section allows supervisors to manage and review the leave requests submitted by their assigned employees. You can view the list of pending, approved, and rejected leave applications here.

**How to Access:**
- Navigate to the **Employee Request** section from your dashboard.
- Here you will see a table listing all the latest leave applications submitted by the employees under your supervision.

![Employee Leave Request Supervisor](/img/hr/employee-deshboard/employee-request/Employee-request-list.png)

---
## Employee Request Click Approved or Reject Button

When an employee submits a new leave request, it awaits your evaluation:
1. Locate the pending leave request in the list.
2. In the action column, click on the button to review the entry.
3. A dialog or options menu will appear containing the **Approve** and **Reject** buttons to finalize the decision.

![Employee Leave Request Click Approved or Reject Button](/img/hr/employee-deshboard/employee-request/Employee-request-approved-regect.png)

---
## Employee Leave Request Supervisor Approve

If you decide to grant the leave request to the employee:
1. Click the **Approve** button next to the request.
2. (Optional) Provide any necessary remarks or confirm the prompt if asked.
3. Once completed, the leave request status will be updated to **Approved**, and the employee's remaining leave balance will be automatically adjusted.

![Employee Leave Request Supervisor Approve](/img/hr/employee-deshboard/employee-request/Employee-request-supervisor-approved.png)

---
## Employee Leave Request All Approve (Approval Workflow)

The leave approval process follows a strict two-step workflow to ensure proper management oversight:

1. **Supervisor Approval**: First, the direct supervisor reviews and approves the employee's leave request under this section.
2. **HR Final Approval**: Once approved by the supervisor, the application is forwarded to the HR department for final confirmation.
3. **Leave Granted**: The employee's leave request is only considered fully approved (and leave balance deducted) after both the Supervisor and HR have signed off on it.

This process guarantees that both team operations and company policies are respected before a leave is finalized.

![Employee Leave Request All Approve](/img/hr/employee-deshboard/employee-request/Employee-request-all-approved.png)

---
## Employee Leave Request Supervisor Reject

If you cannot grant the leave request (e.g., due to a busy work schedule or invalid reason):
1. Click the **Reject** button.
2. You might be prompted to provide a rejection reason so the employee understands why their leave was turned down.
3. Once confirmed, the request status instantly updates to **Rejected**. 
4. The final table list will clearly reflect all updated statuses, distinguishing between pending, approved, and rejected applications.

![Employee Leave Request Supervisor Reject](/img/hr/employee-deshboard/employee-request/Employee-request-list-Hr-approved-reject.png)
