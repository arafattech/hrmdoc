# My Roster

The **My Roster** section offers a clear view of an employee's assigned work shifts over the month.

## Viewing Your Roster

To check your shift schedule, follow these steps:

1. Click on the **Employee Dashboard** from the left-hand sidebar menu.
2. Select the **My Roster** tab from the upper navigation menu.

**Features of the Roster View:**
- **Shift Details Panel (Left side):** Displays a legend of all possible shifts assigned to you (e.g., `P/A`, `P/B`, `P/C`, `P/D`), along with their corresponding times and color codes.
- **Roster Table (Right side):** Shows a horizontal calendar view for your scheduled work.
  - **Month Selection:** Use the dropdown (e.g., *APRIL*) to switch between different months.
  - **Date Timeline:** Scroll horizontally using the left/right arrows to see the shift designated under specific dates (e.g., P/A on Sunday, P/C on Monday).
