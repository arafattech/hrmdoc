The **Payroll** section allows employees to view and download their salary breakdowns and payroll history.

## Payroll List

To check your payroll history, follow these steps:

1. Go to the **Employee Dashboard** via the left sidebar menu.
2. Click on the **Payroll** tab from the top navigation.
3. You will see the **Employee Payroll** table displaying your past salary records.

**Features of the Payroll List:**
- **Search bar:** Easily locate specific payroll records.
- **Filter Options:** Sort the list by clicking **All**, **Active**, or **Inactive**.
- **Table Data:** View key columns such as `Basic Salary`, `Total deductions`, `Bonus`, `Allowance`, `Payment Status`, `Payment Method`, `Process Date`, `Hr Notes`, and `Net Salary`.
- **Actions:** Use the action icons to **View Details** (eye icon) or **Download** (download icon) your salary slip.

![Payroll](/img/hr/employee-deshboard/payroll-list.png)

## Payroll View

To view detailed information about a specific payroll:

1. Click the **View (eye)** icon in the Actions column of the desired payroll record.
2. A **Details** modal will appear showing an in-depth breakdown of your salary logic.
3. **Data Displayed:** The pop-up includes fields like `Total Attendance`, `Payable Days`, `Total Deductions`, `Net Salary`, and more.
4. Review the information and click **Cancel** to close the detailed view window.
