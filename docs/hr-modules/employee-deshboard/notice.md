# Notice

The **Notice** tab offers a central repository where employees can read official company announcements and important internal memos.

## Notice List

To access company notices, follow these steps:

1. Access the **Employee Dashboard** via the left sidebar navigation menu.
2. Click on the **Notice** tab in the main top navigation.
3. You will see a **Notice List** table detailing all distributed announcements.

**Features of the Notice List:**
- **Search bar:** Find a specific notice efficiently using the top right filter box.
- **Table Data:** View basic details like `Title` and `Created On` date.
- **Actions:** To read an entire announcement, click the **Info (i)** icon located in the Actions column.

![Notice List](/img/hr/employee-deshboard/notice.png)

## Viewing a Notice

If you want to read the full body of an announcement:

1. Click the **Actions (Info)** button on the specific notice row.
2. A **Details** modal will pop up containing the full notice.
3. The modal will display the notice **Title** and a **Content** textbox containing the body of the memo (e.g., "The office will remain closed...").
4. If required, click the **"I Have Read"** check box.
5. Click **Cancel** to close the window once you've finished reading.
