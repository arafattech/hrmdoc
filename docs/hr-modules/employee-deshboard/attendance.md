# Attendance

The **Attendance** tab allows employees to view their day-to-day check-in and check-out records and total working hours.

## Attendance List

To check your attendance history, follow these steps:

1. From the left sidebar menu, access the **Employee Dashboard**.
2. Click on the **Attendance** tab located on the top navigation bar.
3. You will be presented with the **Attendance Lists** table which summarizes your time logs.

**Features of the Attendance List:**
- **Search bar:** Use the top-right search box to find attendance on specific dates or shifts.
- **Table Data:** View columns covering `Employee No`, `Employee Name`, `Attendance Date`, `Check-In Time`, `Check-Out Time`, `Working Hour`, and `Shift`.
- **Actions:** Use the tools available in the Actions column to **Edit** (pencil icon) or **Delete** (trash icon) attendance logs, if applicable and permitted.
