# My Profile

The **My Profile** section acts as the comprehensive landing page for an employee's dashboard, displaying their personal overview, attendance summary, and detailed employment data.

## Employee Profile Overview

To view your profile information, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to the **Employee Dashboard** utilizing the left sidebar menu. By default, it opens the **My Profile** tab.

**Features of the Profile Dashboard:**
- **Left Panel (Overview):** Shows your photo, Name, ID, Designation, and Email. Below the standard info, you can view summary cards showcasing:
  - `Check-In Time` & `Check-out Time`
  - `Total Salary` & `Total Present` days value
  - `Leave Summary` metrics (Medical Leave, Total Leave, etc.)
- **Right Panel (Employee Details):** Displays your in-depth demographic and system information, covering `Contact`, `Gender`, `Department`, `Blood Group`, `Religion`, `Shift`, `Supervisor`, `NID Number`, `Emergency Contact`, `Date of Birth`, and `Joining Date`.

## Changing Password

To update your account password directly from your profile, follow these steps:

1. Click on the **Settings (gear)** icon located at the top right corner of the **Employee Details** card.
2. A **Settings** modal will pop up. 
3. **Current Password:** Enter your existing password in the first field. This is mandatory (`*`).
4. **New Password:** Enter your new chosen password. This is mandatory (`*`).
5. **Confirm Password:** Re-enter the new password to confirm. This is mandatory (`*`).
6. Click the blue **Submit** button to save changes, or click **Cancel** to abort the password reset.
