# My Calendar

The **My Calendar** tab displays a monthly schedule overview highlighting important dates, holidays, or events.

## Using the Calendar

To navigate your calendar, follow these steps:

1. Go to the **Employee Dashboard** via the left sidebar menu.
2. Select the **My Calendar** tab from the top navigation bar.

**Features of My Calendar:**
- **Monthly Grid View:** The center screen displays a large monthly grid (e.g., *April 2026*), with days of the week grouped in columns (SUN to SAT).
- **Navigation Controls:** Use the **Prev** and **Next** buttons at the top right to switch between previous and upcoming months.
- **Today Button:** Click the **Today** button to quickly snap the view back to the current month and day.
- **Event Highlights:** Special dates, leaves, or company events will be marked across the calendar grid.
