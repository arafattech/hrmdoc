# Designation

The **Designation** module allows you to define and manage all the official job titles or roles (e.g., Developer, Manager, Officer) available in your organization. These designations are assigned to employees to define their position in the company hierarchy.

## Viewing Designations

The dashboard provides a list of all current designations:

- **Active**: A checkmark indicates if the designation is currently active and can be assigned to employees.
- **Name**: The title of the designation.
- **Created On**: The date the designation was added to the system.
- **Actions**: Options to **Edit** or **Delete** the designation.

Use the search bar to find a specific designation quickly, or filter the list using the **All**, **Active**, and **Inactive** tabs.

![Designation Overview](/img/hr/designation/designation-list.png)

## Adding a Designation

To create a new designation:

1. Click the **New** button at the top right.
2. In the "**New**" modal:
   - Keep the **Active** toggle enabled if the designation should be immediately available.
   - Enter the **Designation Name** (e.g., "Developer").
3. Click **Submit** to save.

![Add Designation](/img/hr/designation/designation-add.png)

## Editing a Designation

To modify an existing designation:

1. Click the **Edit** icon next to the designation.
2. Update the designation's name or toggle its active status.
3. Click **Submit** to apply the changes.

![Edit Designation](/img/hr/designation/designation-edit.png)

## Managing Inactive Designations

Designations no longer in use can be marked as inactive. To view or reactivate them:

1. Click on the **Inactive** tab at the top right.
2. To reactivate, click the **Edit** icon, enable the **Active** toggle, and click **Submit**.

![Inactive Designation List](/img/hr/designation/designation-inactive-list.png)

## Deleting a Designation

To permanently remove a designation:

1. Click the **Delete** icon next to the designation.
2. A warning modal will prompt: *"Are you sure you want to delete?"*
3. Click **Delete** to confirm. 

*Note: Be cautious when deleting a designation if it is currently assigned to active employees.*

![Delete Designation](/img/hr/designation/designation-delete.png)
