# Asset List

The **Asset List** module provides a centralized view of all company assets and their current assignment status. This module allows HR and management to track who has been issued specific equipment, the duration of use, and any relevant notes.

![Asset Management Overview](/img/hr/asset-management/asset-list-full-screen.png)

## Viewing Assets

The main dashboard displays a list of all recorded assets.

- **S/N**: Serial number for listing purposes.
- **Asset Name**: The name or type of the asset (e.g., Computer, Laptop).
- **Created On**: The date the asset was first registered in the system.

### Searching for Assets
You can quickly find a specific asset by using the **Search** bar at the top right of the table.

## Asset Assignment Details

Selecting an asset from the list will open a side panel (or split view) showing the history of employees who have used or are currently using that asset.

![Asset Employee Assignment](/img/hr/asset-management/asset-employee-assign.png)

### Employees Using Asset List
The panel on the right displays the following details for the selected asset:

- **ID**: The unique employee ID.
- **Employee Name**: The name of the employee the asset is/was assigned to.
- **Issue Date**: The date the asset was handed over to the employee.
- **Return Date**: The date the asset was returned (if applicable).
- **Actions**: Click the **Info** icon to view more detailed remarks.

![Asset Assign Employee Full Screen](/img/hr/asset-management/asset-assign-employee-full-screen.png)

## Viewing Assignment Remarks

To view more context about a specific assignment, click the **Info** icon in the Actions column.

![Asset Assign Employee View](/img/hr/asset-management/asset-asign-employee-view.png)

The **Details** modal will display:
1. **Asset Name**: Confirms the item being discussed.
2. **Employee Name**: Name of the staff member.
3. **Note**: Any special instructions or conditions at the time of issue (e.g., "Please return after resign").
4. **Dates**: Clear visibility of both **Issued Date** and **Return Date**.
5. **Remark**: Additional comments (e.g., "Office Purpose").

:::info
Use this view to verify the condition or purpose of an asset assignment during audits or employee offboarding.
:::
